Welcome To Government Polytechnic Unnao

Government Polytechnic Unnao was established in the year of 1984. It is situated on National Highway – 25 at a distance of 5 km. from Unnao Jn. Railway station. The present campus measured 7 Acres of land.

The institution is running 5 courses in Computer Science and Engineering , Electronics Engineering , Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Application , Post Graduate Diploma in Hardware and Networking , Post Graduate Diploma in Accountancy with intake of 60 students in each.

Government Polytechnic Unnao is approved by AICTE Govt. of India and affiliated to Board of Technical Education UP (BTEUP) .

The College has fully furnished air conditioned Computer Lab equipped with Broadband Internet facility and video projection facility.

Exploring Engineering Branches

Computer Science and Engineering

Explore the dynamic world of computing, algorithms, and software development. Computer Science and Engineering focus on creating innovative solutions, from software applications to system architectures.

P.G. Diploma in Computer Application

Acquire comprehensive knowledge in computer applications and software development. This diploma program equips you with skills in programming, database management, and application development.

P.G. Diploma in Cyber Security

Specialize in protecting digital systems from cyber threats. This program provides hands-on training in cybersecurity strategies, ethical hacking, and incident response.

P.G. Diploma in Computer Hardware & Networking

Develop skills in computer hardware architecture and network management. This diploma program covers the installation, maintenance, and troubleshooting of computer systems and networks.

P.G. Diploma in Accountancy (with Computerized Account & Taxation)

Gain expertise in accounting practices, financial management, and taxation. This program integrates computerized accounting techniques, preparing you for a role at the intersection of finance and technology.

Electronics Engineering

Delve into the intricacies of electronic circuits, communication systems, and semiconductor devices. Electronics Engineering focuses on creating, testing, and maintaining electronic systems critical to modern technology.
